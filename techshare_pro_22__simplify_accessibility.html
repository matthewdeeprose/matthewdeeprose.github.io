Making accessibility simpler in Higher Education and beyond!

A "Lunch and Learn" session at TechSharePro 2022 delivered on 16 November 2022.

Presentation recording and transcript

Play Video: Making accessibility simpler in Higher Education and beyond!

Read the transcript

Synopsis

Let's review three approaches to making accessibility more approachable for busy colleagues by answering these three questions:

  • How can we demystify alternative text for complex images like charts and graphs?
  • How can we simplify accessible brand palette colour choices?
  • How can we amplify engagement with accessibility?

While this session is based on experiences within the Higher Education sector, they are applicable in other organisations.
