About

How can we make sense of digital accessibility regulations and their application within our institutions?

On this journey to learn more about accessibility, I attempt to share knowledge and experiences through delivering presentations, writing blog posts, and other projects. I still have much to learn and am grateful to the support of the wide and friendly digital accessibility community.

The best way to get in touch with me is through connecting on LinkedIn.

I've built this site from scratch using Notepad ++. My aim is to create a simple, fast, and responsive site that is as compliant as possible with WCAG 2.1.
