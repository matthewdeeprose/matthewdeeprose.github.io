Contrast Companion

What is this tool?

This tool helps you find colours that have sufficient contrast when paired with your existing palette. Simply input your current colour set, and the tool will identify complementary colours that meet accessibility standards across your entire palette.

Input Colours
Enter 6-character hex colour codes (with or without # prefix)

CSV format: One colour per line or comma-separated

JSON format: {"paletteName": ["#colour1", "#colour2", ...]}
